## current position

**Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science**  
[Universidad Industrial de Santander](https://uis.edu.co/) · Bucaramanga, Colombia

Research in computational imaging, spectral imaging, compressive sensing, inverse problems, and deep learning, with an emphasis on jointly optimizing acquisition systems and data-driven models.

## research experience

### Computational and spectral imaging

- End-to-end optical encoder design.
- Hyperspectral band selection for classification and segmentation.
- Diffusion and generative models for compressive image reconstruction.
- Spectral image generation and RGB-to-spectral reconstruction.
- Computational calibration and measurement augmentation.
- Efficient acquisition strategies for practical spectral imaging systems.
